What are the types of review?
1.) Informal Review: No formal process, just a quick review check.
		     provide fast feedback on the product.

2.) Walkthrough: discuss with team memebers about how system works
		 feedback comes from whole team side

3.) Technical Review: review conducted by technical experts with technical aspects
	      	      focus on only technical quality is met or not
	 
4.) Inspection: final go through of system.
